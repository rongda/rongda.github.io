Love Flower

###Leave the sleep and let the springtime talk
自梦乡苏醒聆听春天的呼唤

###LIn tongues from the time before men
自久于人类的时间暗示中

###LListen to a daffodil tell her tale
听水仙倾诉属于自己的童话

###LLet the guest in, walk out
邀同伴偕行

###LBe the first to greet the morn
定要第一个迎接破晓

###LThe meadows of heaven await harvest
天堂之甸静待收割

###LThe cliffs unjumped, cold waters untouched
未曾飞跃的山崖，未曾触碰的寒泉

###LThe elsewhere creatures yet unseen
未曾发现的珍禽异兽

###LFinally you number came up
同伴既至

###LFree fall awaits the brave.
飞瀑等待勇者

###LCome, taste the wine, race the blind
来，干了这美酒，无所顾忌地飞驰

###LThey will guide you from the light
他们就在远方微亮处引导着你

###LWriting noughts till the end of time
肆意狂书直到时间也凝固

###LCome, surf the clouds, race the dark
来，以浮云为舟，以黑暗为海

###LIt feeds from the runs undone
从无虑的驰骋中得到释放

###LMeet me where the cliff greets the sea
在海崖那边我等待与你的邂逅
